Hello! I am a Master's student in Political Science at IESP/UERJ and hold a BA in Political Science from UNIRIO. My research focuses on Brazilian and South American politics, with emphasis on presidentialism, legislative studies, executive-legislative relations, and government coalitions. My research agenda combines institutional analysis and empirical approaches, with growing emphasis on quantitative methodology, legislative data analysis, and statistical models applied to Political Science.

Atualmente pesquiso no OPSA/IESP, no NECON/IESP e no GRISUL/UNIRIO. Também sou editor do Conjuntura Latitude Sul, periódico mensal do OPSA.

I currently research at OPSA/IESP, NECON/IESP, and GRISUL/UNIRIO. I also serve as editor of Conjuntura Latitude Sul, a monthly journal from OPSA.
